About Us

Since 2009, KS Refrigerator Service has been your trusted partner in appliance repair. With expertise spanning over a decade, we specialize in servicing a wide range of appliances, including all types of fridges (Single Door, Double Door, Side-by-Side, and Western Door), washing machines (old, new, and automatic), ovens (from bakery ovens to microwave ovens), and Split ACs. Our commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction ensures your appliances are in good hands, bringing them back to optimal performance with precision and care.
